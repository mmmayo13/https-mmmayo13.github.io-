Matthew Mayo is a data scientist and the Editor-in-Chief of KDnuggets, a leading site on data science, machine learning, AI and analytics. Matthew is also the managing editor of Data Science Horizons. With undergraduate and graduate degrees in computer science, he brings over 10 years of experience in the data science field. Matthew has contributed to numerous data science projects and publications and been with KDnuggets for 8 years, having taken over editing duties from the website's founder, Gregory Piatetsky, in January 2022.

In addition to his passion for data science, Matthew is also an avid comic book collector and hip hop music aficionado. He is a dedicated husband and father and loves to travel the world.
